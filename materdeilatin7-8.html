10 November 2016

Do Pensum A (pp. 30-31) again and bring a written copy to class. Don't forget to include your name on your paper! As you redo Pensum A, think carefully about context. For each blank, ask yourself: Is this a noun or a verb? If it's a noun, what case should it be and why? If it's a verb, is it indicative or imperative? Don't just write an ending that sounds right to you; you should be able to give a reasoned answer to explain what you wrote.
