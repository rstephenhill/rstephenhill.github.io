Mater Dei Schole Latin (grades 3-6)

For parents: We're using a combination of Lingua Latina: Familia Romana and other materials for elementary school Latin. This is partly because Lingua Latina moves rather quickly and partly because more variety tends to retain students' interest better than Lingua Latina alone. For those of you who wish to do more at home, I've provided some optional activities and videos. Nota bene (note well): the exercises at wyomingcatholic.net cannot be automatically checked without a Wyoming Catholic email address. For this reason, if you'd like feedback on them, just print the page after your child has done the exercise (to save printer paper, select the text of the exercise and print "selected text only") or copy the answers on a sheet of paper. Have your child bring the paper to class and I'll return it the following week. If you have any questions, please feel free to email me at rstephenhill at gmail dot com.
