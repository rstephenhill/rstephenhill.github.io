NOTECARD INSTRUCTIONS

Each week, turn in one 4x6 notecard. Your notecard should include your name, the date, a quote from the week's reading assignment, and a short paragraph containing an analysis of something that intrigued you from the reading. This analysis may have a broad or narrow focus: for example, you could focus on a theme that runs through the whole week's reading or through the entire work, or you could focus on a few lines that piqued your interest. The following week, I will return the notecards to you. You may receive a "check" ✔ (for a satisfactory quote and analysis), a "check plus" ✔+ (for a satisfactory quote and an especially insightful analysis), or a "check minus" ✔- (for a less than satisfactory quote and analysis).
