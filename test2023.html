Συνουσία θερεία περὶ τὰ ἑλληνικὰ

συνίωμεν ὡς ἑλληνιστὶ διαλεξόμενοι

When:

  • Saturday, June 17th, 2023
  • Six hours of sessions (3 in the morning, 3 in the afternoon)

Where:

Online.

Prerequisites:

Some experience speaking ancient Greek and at least two, preferably more, years of formal instruction in the language.

 

Cost:

$150 per participant.

Facilitator:

Co-founder of the Lexington σύνοδος ἑλληνική (2017-19), Stephen Hill has taught ancient Greek communicatively for over ten years in Rome, Florida, Virginia, and Wyoming. He is Instructor of Latin at Wyoming Catholic College and a PhD candidate in Classics at the University of Virginia. Most recently, he has published (with Christophe Rico) a translation of Hansel and Gretel into ancient Greek (Polis Institute Press).
