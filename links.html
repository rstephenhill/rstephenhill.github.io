Online Lexica

Liddell, Scott, and Jones (LSJ): the standard Greek-English lexicon.
Dictionnaire étymologique de la langue grecque by Pierre Chantraine.
Diccionario Griego-Español: in progress; so far entries are available from α to ἔξαυος.
Woodhouse's Greek-English dictionary: Use with caution! Always check against LSJ.
Logeion at UChicago: a convenient interface for accessing a variety of lexica.
Diogenes online. It can also be downloaded here.
A variety of older lexica, including Greek-Latin and Latin-Greek.

Print Lexica

The standard English lexicon of the New Testament is BDAG (2001) (BMCR review). A much smaller (and more affordable) version was published in 2007. For patristic Greek, the standard work is the preposterously expensive Lampe. At one point, there was a PDF of the 1961 edition on archive.org.
